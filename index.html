Find out your Akan Name!

Today you shall find out your Akan name whether your male or female

Akan names come from old African naming systems located in West Africa from countries such as Ghana and Ivory Coast

factors to be considered when giving a child an Akan name are;

  1. The day the child was born
  2. The gender of the child
  3. Each child was born with a name associated to their soul
  4. Rank among siblings
